Recent Academic Projects (2019-20)

The following sections contain links to my papers, project reports and slides in .pdf format.

Machine Learning
  • Determination of life expectancy using linear regression. Key factors that affect life expectancy of an individual determined with statistical significance. March 2020.
  • Classification of US income using Logistic Regression, LDA, Boosting, Decision trees and SVM. The probability that an average individual falls under a given income class is determined using foresaid methods. April 2020.
  • Clustering US states for predicting patterns in the COVID-19 data, using PCA, hierarchical and K-means clustering. Meaningful inferences derived for strategy and policy implementation. View the inference slides here. April 2020.
  • Clustering US states based on crime rate using PAM algorithm. April 2020.
  • Determination of basic reproduction number of COVID-19 using SIR compartmental model. Notes on mathematical models in epidemiology. April 2020.
  • Determinants and Permanents of a matrix. And their connection to graph theory and computationally complex P-NP problem. May 2020.
Finance and Microeconomics
  • On the Weak Axiom of Revealed Preference, consistency and motives in buying behavior. An experimental study of buying behavior based on a similar work by Harbaugh. September 2019.
  • The five-factor Fama French Model. Notes on the extension of the asset-pricing model CAPM based on the 2015 paper by Fama and French. January 2020.
Organizational Behavior and HRM
  • What motivates employees to gossip? A literature review on organizational gossip, its advantages and demerits. May 2020.
  • Salary Inequities. A case study on salary inequities in the US and a review of psychological causes, ethical and sustainable solutions to the same. May 2020.
  • On causes and spread of xenophobia during the COVID-19 pandemic. A survey based investigation on xenophobia & racism and possible psychological roots to deviance behavior. April 2020.
Marketing Management
  • Marketing Strategy for the Madras School of Economics. Developing a marketing roadmap for the PGDM program at MSE. October 2019.
  • Netflix' Marketing Strategy. Analyzing the marketing tools employed by Netflix in attracting customers, given the steady growth in rival platforms. September 2019
